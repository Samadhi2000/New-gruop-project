Bambarakanda falls




.Bambarakanda Falls (Bambarakale Falls) is the highest waterfall in Sri Lanka.It ranks 299th among the tallest waterfalls in the world.Its height is 263 m (863 ft).
This is located in Kalupahana area of ​​Badulla. Located 5km away from the A4 road.
  The months of March to May is the best time to visit Bambarakanda Falls.
This is the time when this region receives moderate rainfall. October to February runs the monsoon season and the waterfall looks absolutely spellbinding.
But this time is not considered to be very safe for a visit. So, make sure you follow the safety instructions if you happen to visit in Bambarakanda Falls during this season.
June to September is the Summer season in this region and also an off-season for a visit. The weather is dry and so is the waterfall, during this time.
